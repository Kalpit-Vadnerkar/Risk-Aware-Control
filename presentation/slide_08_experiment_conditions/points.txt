PHASE 2 — FAULT ROBUSTNESS
Goal: Compute confidence C(fault_type, magnitude) = P(correct classification | fault)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Camera fault — corrupts traffic light state recognition:

  Base scenario     | Fault                  | Magnitude | Purpose
  ------------------|------------------------|-----------|--------------------------------------
  nominal           | TL state dropout       | 20%       | Baseline signal under mild camera fault
  nominal           | TL state dropout       | 60%       | Baseline signal under severe camera fault
  obs_recovery      | TL state dropout       | 20%       | Avoidance detection — mild camera fault
  obs_recovery      | TL state dropout       | 60%       | Avoidance detection — severe camera fault

  IMU fault — corrupts ego state estimates (position/velocity/heading) fed to ST-GAT:

  Base scenario     | Fault                  | Magnitude      | Purpose
  ------------------|------------------------|----------------|--------------------------------------
  nominal           | IMU additive noise     | σ = 0.05 rad/s | Baseline signal under mild IMU noise
  nominal           | IMU additive noise     | σ = 0.25 rad/s | Baseline signal under severe IMU noise
  obs_recovery      | IMU additive noise     | σ = 0.05 rad/s | Avoidance detection — mild IMU noise
  obs_recovery      | IMU additive noise     | σ = 0.25 rad/s | Avoidance detection — severe IMU noise

  Phase 1 + Phase 2 combined covers:
  → Two road topologies (multi-lane, single-lane)
  → Two avoidance policy states (auto, manual)
